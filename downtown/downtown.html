Must Visit Restaurants!

Luce Pasta

Ciao! A literal hole in the wall under a Downtown street light, Luce offers a small, perfect menu of pastas -- rolled, filled, and cut by hand -- along with a selection of extras, including fired pizzas, kale salad, and the best cheesecake ever (No, really).

Mudhouse

Charlottesville's home of the super fine espresso drinks and coffees roasted with care and brewed with love for maximum happiness.

Kilwins

Our values are simple; Treat others as you want to be treated, Do your best, and Have fun! Coupled with our high quality products, in-store craftsmanship, and genuinely friendly staff we create an atmosphere that our customers want to visit again and again.

Maru

Maru, which is an idiom for "a family room to eat and gather" in Korean, is a modern take on traditional Korean food! Come and enjoy some delicious dishes with us!

Melting Pot

At The Melting Pot, fondue truly becomes a memorable four-course dining experience where patrons can dip into something different - and discover all the ingredients for a unique dining experience including a relaxed atmosphere, private tables, attentive service, fine wines and signature fondue dinners.

Thai Fresh

Tucked just off Charlottesville’s Downtown Mall, Thai Fresh is a local favorite for fast, flavorful, and affordable Thai cuisine. From classic pad thai to rich red curry, every dish is made fresh with care. Open Monday through Saturday, with dine-in, takeout, and online ordering available—perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!

TING PAVILION

Keep up to date with events at Ting Pavilion!

The Ting Pavilion is an outdoor amphitheater located in downtown Charlottesville, Virginia on the historic Downtown Mall. It serves as a premier entertainment venue, hosting live concerts, community events, and performances throughout the year. Originally known as the Sprint Pavilion, it was renamed to Ting Pavilion when Ting became its naming rights sponsor. The venue features a distinctive white tensile roof structure and has become a cultural landmark in Charlottesville's vibrant downtown area.
