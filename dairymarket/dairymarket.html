Must Visit Restaurants!

Basta Pasta

Basta Pasta offers heavenly homemade pasta dishes. Basta Pasta serves a variety of homemade/artisanal pastas made to order with a great selection of different shapes and sizes, protein options, and a large variety of sauces, including vegan and gluten-free options.

Dinos

Dino’s features a state-of-the-art wood oven creating delicious pizzas and Italian treats. Dino’s mission is to be community-driven, pro-local, and eco-friendly—a casual meeting spot where diversity is celebrated, and healthy food made with fresh ingredients is served.

I am Thai

At the I Am Thai food stall, our chefs will craft meals that are reminiscent of the hustle and bustle of the street markets of Thailand. Convenience plays a huge role in Thailand’s food popularity and tradition and the communal aspect of eating are part of the ultimate Thai experience.

Currylicious

Here in Currylicious, we serve the flavors from Nepal and India together to create a unique and unforgettable food experience. Our menu is a celebration of the diverse flavors and spices that define Nepali and Indian cooking.

Maizal

The Charlottesville outpost of Maizal will specialize in Latin American Street Food offering a wide range of punchy, bold Latin American flavors. From arepas that are grilled and stuffed to order to perfectly golden empanadas, a trip to Maizal will satisfy your each and every street craving.

Mashu Festival

Led by Executive Chef TK Chin, Mashu’s menu offers authentic Asian festival food in a fun and decorative atmosphere. The dishes, carefully crafted utilizing the team’s deep knowledge of Japanese cuisine and fresh, traditional ingredients, will ensure diners experience a true taste of Japanese culture.

Moo Thru

Moo Thru has been serving delicious treats since it is first store opened in Remington in the summer of 2010. What was originally intended to be a small outlet to sell the family’s fresh dairy products quickly turned into a busy delicious mid-point rest stop between Charlottesville and DC.

The Sizzle Shack

Come get your hands on some sizzling menu items like the Shack’s Smash—a double-stacked, flavor-packed burger with crispy fries on the side. Bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and Dutch-inspired dipping sauces await.

EVENTS

Keep up to date with events at Dairy Market!

The Dairy Market, housed in Charlottesville's historic Monticello Dairy building, is a modern food hall featuring local vendors serving diverse cuisines from pizza to Korean fusion. This renovated 1930s dairy facility on Preston Avenue offers communal dining space, craft beer, and regular community events, making it a popular gathering spot for locals and visitors alike.
